Let's play Pig Dice!

How to Play

  1. Add players using the "Create a new player" button.
  2. Once you're done adding players, click "Done creating players!" to start the game.
  3. While it's their turn, each player gets unlimited rolls of the die until they either roll a 1 or they click the "Hold your score" button. Each roll adds to a total score for the turn.
    1. If a player rolls a 1, their turn ends and their score does not increase.
    2. If a player chooses to hold their score, their turn ends and their overall score increases by the turn score.
  4. The first player whose overall score reaches 100 or over wins!
